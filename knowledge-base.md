## Meta Ads Performance Issues & Strategic Responses

### Current Performance Crisis (March 2026)
- **Major platform instability** reported across multiple agencies starting mid-February 2026
- **Massive drop in conversion quality** with campaigns showing 3x ROAS now failing to break even
- **Algorithm disruption** affecting stable campaigns overnight with:
  - CPC increases without performance justification
  - Normal CTR but severely reduced conversion rates
  - Same creatives/audiences producing dramatically worse results

### Recommended Actions for Home Services Campaigns
1. **Pause underperforming Meta campaigns** rather than burning budget during platform instability
2. **Reallocate budget to Google Ads/LSA** during Meta recovery period
3. **Plan campaign re-entry strategy** after 7-14 day pause to allow algorithm reset

## Google Ads Optimization for Home Services

### Retargeting Efficiency Warnings
- **Homepage retargeting waste**: Major brands showing ads to users already on their own website
- **Action Required**: Exclude homepage visitors from display retargeting to prevent budget waste on existing traffic

### Pixel Tracking Challenges (2026)
- **Consent banner defaults** now set to "Denied" causing cookieless tracking
- **Enhanced Conversions accuracy** significantly reduced
- **Search term modeling** showing irrelevant conversions
- **Solution**: Implement first-party data collection and server-side tracking where possible

## Platform Risk Management

### Meta Account Security Issues
- **Staff account bans** increasing across agencies without clear cause
- **Personal profile risks** for team members managing client accounts
- **Zero appeal success** rate for banned accounts requiring proactive protection

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
1. **Backup account access** through multiple team members
2. **Business Manager separation** from personal profiles where possible
3. **Budget diversification** across Google and Meta to reduce platform dependency
4. **Documentation protocols** for account recovery attempts
